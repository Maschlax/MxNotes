MxNotes - Privacy Policy

This privacy policy outlines how we interact with your personal information when you use our MxNotes application.

Information We Collect

MxNotes is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All your notes and data are stored locally on your device using your browser's local storage.

How Your Data is Stored

All notes and application preferences are stored entirely in your browser's local storage. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • We have no access to your notes or personal information
  • Your data persists between sessions but can be cleared through your browser settings

Third-Party Services

MxNotes does not integrate with any third-party services or analytics platforms. We don't use cookies or tracking mechanisms of any kind.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally, the security of your notes depends on your device's security. We recommend:

  • Keeping your browser updated
  • Using secure device passwords
  • Clearing your browser data when using shared devices

Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy as needed. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please visit our GitHub repository or contact us through our website.
